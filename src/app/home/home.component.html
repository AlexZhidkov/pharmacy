FROM PACK -> COLLECT

Webbit tracks all packs through:

  • Packing
  • Checking
  • Collecting
  • Delivery

NO MORE BOTTLENECKS

Heaps to pack.. or check? Allocate your staff to the correct area with a live chart so you can stay on top of progress every day.

DELIVERIES

Patients can be toggled to be delivery patients. When packs are checked they are added to a delivery list which is available to print out.

NO MORE PAPER

Throw out your paper logbooks and trust a cloud system that runs on all computers. Staff on the front counter can access Webbit, find pack locations and assist patients with minimal fuss or confusion.

STAFF AUDIT

All status changes have a staff ID connected registered with them. Staff are given permission to Pack, Check or Collect depending upon their position in the pharmacy.

ANALYSE WORKLOAD

Analyse your workload by getting live reports on how many packs are within each status so you can organise labour more efficiently.
